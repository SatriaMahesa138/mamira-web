Hot Spring

Enjoy the beauty of Bali from above during this tour of Mount Batur and its surroundings! Mount Batur is an active volcano located in the Bangli region on the island of Bali, Indonesia. Although relatively calm at the moment, the volcano has erupted several times in the past, resulting in extensive black lava spilling down the volcano's slopes.

Diamond Beach

A hidden paradise in Bali located on Nusa Penida Island. This beach is famous for the beauty of its fine white sand, clear sea water and towering coral cliffs around it. It gets its name from its jewel-like shape, with white sand that shines in the sun.
